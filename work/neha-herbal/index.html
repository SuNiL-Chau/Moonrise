Neha Herbals

Branding

Identity Design

Packaging Design

Neha Herbals is a pioneer in herbal personal and hair care, specializing in mehendi—its best-selling product. Committed to quality and sustainability, the brand also offers a range of hair care products and gentle handwashes, all crafted from the finest natural ingredients to promote wellness and beauty.

Neha Herbals partnered with Studio Moonrise to elevate their hair color branding. Together, we redesigned their packaging with an elegant and modern look that highlights the natural quality of their products. This fresh identity enhances shelf appeal and reinforces Neha Herbals commitment to herbal beauty, positioning them as a standout choice in the hair color market.

We revitalized Neha Herbals’ hair color packaging across six variants, introducing a cohesive and contemporary design system. The new packaging features an elegant, modern aesthetic that resonates with current trends. Key herbal ingredients—hibiscus, amla, and henna—are beautifully depicted with vibrant visuals and clean layouts.

The new packaging features a prominent hair swatch, helping consumers visualize results. Removing the model and adding modern golden accents gives the design a more premium, elegant feel, aligning with contemporary standards.

The redesigned packaging for Neha Herbals hair color variants features a sleek and modern box that effectively showcases the specific hair color, ensuring easy recognition. The packaging offers a premium shelf experience with its vibrant colors, glossy finish , creating an eye-catching display.

Transforming Ideas into Eye-Catching Amazon Banners and Posters!

We designed informative and engaging posters and Amazon banners for Neha Herbals’ hair color category. Each poster highlights product details, benefits, and ingredients, educating consumers on the brand’s quality and sustainability. With clear messaging and organized layouts, these visuals captivate and build trust, guiding customers to make informed choices in herbal personal care.
